Frequently Asked Questions

Who can attend the sessions? +
Our mentorship program is open to students from Class 6 to Class 12 across all streams and boards. We also offer specialized guidance for competitive exam preparation.
Are the sessions free? +
We offer one free introductory session for all new students. After that, we have affordable packages to continue your mentorship journey based on your needs.
How long is each session? +
Each mentorship session typically lasts 45-60 minutes. This gives enough time for meaningful discussion while keeping it focused and productive.
What technology do I need? +
You just need a device (computer, tablet or phone) with internet connection. We use secure video conferencing platforms that work on all major browsers and devices.
